Plot Board Game Based on Genghis Khan



Instructions

On your turn

On your turn, you must click the "start turn" button. Roll a die and move the number of spaces. If you pass a tribe, you must stop on it and roll a dice if you roll above 3, your tribes united counter will go up by one. If you roll a 3 or a 2, you will go backwards by one space. If you roll a 1, the tribe will kill you and you will immediately lose the game

How the game ends

If you get to the last space on the board in 10 or less turns, you win, but if you have more than 10 turns, you lose.

How this game is related to Genghis Kahn

Genghis Kahn started as a normal kid named Temujin, he had to bring together many Mongolian tribes to become well known and get the name Genghis Khan.
